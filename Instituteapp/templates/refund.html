Refund Policy

At Bright Future Coaching Institute, we are committed to providing high-quality coaching and ensuring satisfaction for all students. We understand that sometimes circumstances may require a refund, and we handle these requests professionally.

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of enrollment. All requests are reviewed carefully, and processed within 5 business days. Refunds may be partial or full depending on the course, materials, or services utilized.

To request a refund, please contact our support team via Contact Page or email. We strive to respond promptly and assist you in a transparent and fair manner.

Our policies are designed to maintain trust and provide a professional experience. By enrolling, you agree to these terms while we ensure your satisfaction and learning success remain our top priorities.
